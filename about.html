    I make games and write stories that explore neurodivergency, subcultures, Queer & Trans identities, and the spaces that exist between reality and dreams.

    As a games worker for over a decade, I am drawn towards projects and teams which prioritize curiosity, representation, and worker well-being.

    I’m a multidisciplinary storyteller and believe in taking a holistic approach to crafting worlds for audiences to explore. Everyone on a team has something to contribute; I believe that immersive, believable stories are made better by collaborating with a team with diverse voices and experiences.

    As a life-long immersive storyteller, being trusted with effectively delivering an experience is what drives my creativity, whether through a professional production, or whilst reveling in moon-lit woods with my fortune-telling clown character at a festival.

    Outside of games, I work in film as a Script Supervisor. I'm a performance artist, enjoy travelling to new places, crafting light-up props, and editing clothes. 🧦💜
