Cave Drive

Cave Drive is a feature-rich first-person platformer contained in a single level. Explore a detailed environment while solving puzzles, dodging bullets, and figuring out how to secure a reward! You'll find yourself swinging from grapple points, speeding down ziplines, and more.

Created in Unreal Engine 4 over two and a half months, Cave Drive challenges you to explore a subterranean environment, solve puzzles, and make off with a reward.

Deficit

A Semi-Fictional Experiential ADHD story, based on real-life experiences with ADHD. Navigate your way through several vignettes as you learn more about how structural barriers and attitudes can create a difficult environment for those whose brains work differently.

Built in Twine over several months in 2020. Over 7000 plays to date! Featured in HAU4's Digital Stage! Read the Artist Statement here.






Fortune

Fortune is a short first-person experience - consult with a forlorn fortune telling machination, discuss existential subject matter, and perhaps it will deign you worthy of a new inventory item.

Created in Unreal Engine 4 over three months, Fortune was built to showcase skills with branching dialogue systems and scripting. Assets self-made (minus audio + base textures).
